Interest

I really enjoy spending time with my family. I have the most fun with my family when we are doing water events. I really had a lot of fun when my wife and I went flyboarding in Florida. This was the first time and probably the last time that I will ever go flyboarding because it was very scary once I actually did it. If you have never went flyboarding, you basically lock your feet into a skateboard. At the bottom of the board, there are water jets that push you higher and higher into the air. When you are in the air you literally have no way to break your fall and you will smack into the water each and every time. I am glad that I did this but, I do not feel the need to do this again!

There was another time that I can remember that was very fun with my family. This was a time that my wife and I took our kids to a waterpark. My daughter was finally excited to get on the taller water rides and that made me very happy. The time before this, she did not want to even look at the tall water slides because she was too afraid. Of course, I would not force her to go on anything that she didnt want to get on. This time she wanted to get on every tall ride. She even wanted to get on rides that were almost too tall for me, lol! My son is a lot younger and there for he was not ready to get on any of the water slides with us, but he had a blast getting in the kiddie pool. My daughter and I made sure to make sure that he had a great time as well.
